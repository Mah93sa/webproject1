Welcom to the simplestyle_banner

Helpshift includes features for a brand to organize, store, and work with its snippets of translated text. We call this this feature set “text templates”.Click here
Text templates serve brands that use multiple languages while supporting a product. When your brand offers product support services in one language exclusively, text templates may not be right for you.

Helpshift, Inc., is not a translation services vendor.Brands are responsible for obtaining any translated text strings that they would store and use. Only the users with assigned administrator (or supervisor, in some cases) permissions can manage a brand’s text templates. Our platform can render the character sets for more than 180 languages.

The purpose of of a text template is to simplify internationalization of any consumer-facing:
• greeting
• title
• message
• question
• or survey that you may prepare for your brand’s in-app or web-based customer support experience.
